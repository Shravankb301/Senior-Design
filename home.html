The Impact of Artificial Intelligence:

The Future of Programmers with Autocomplete

About Our Project

As technology continues to advance, the role of artificial intelligence (AI) in various fields, including programming, has become a subject of curiosity and speculation. The question of whether AI tools will replace programmers in the future, or if they will serve as helpful tools to enhance their work, has piqued the interest of researchers and practitioners alike. Our research aims to shed light on this topic by exploring the potential impact of AI on the future of programming, and identifying how programmers can leverage AI tools to enhance their work. To gain insights, we conducted a study among Computer Science students from PFW, utilizing a questionnaire that included programming tasks performed with and without the assistance of AI tools, such as ChatGPT-3. By delving into this area, we hope to provide a better understanding of the potential relationship between AI and programming, and shed light on how AI tools can be utilized by programmers in their day-to-day work.
