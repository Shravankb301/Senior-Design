The Impact of Artificial Intelligence:

The Future of Programmers with Autocomplete

About Our Project

The rapid advancement of technology, particularly in Artificial Intelligence (AI), has garnered significant attention and speculation regarding its potential impact on various fields, including programming. The utilization of AI in programming has gained widespread attention due to its potential to automate routine coding tasks, improve coding efficiency, and enhance the overall software development process. Autocomplete tools, powered by AI algorithms, can automatically generate code suggestions based on context and previous code patterns, thereby reducing the need for manual coding efforts. This has raised questions about the potential implications of such AI tools on the role of programmers in the future workforce. [7]

To address this question, our research will investigate various autocomplete tools commonly used in the programming community. We will conduct empirical testing among Computer Science students, who represent the next generation of programmers, to gather their perspectives on the impact of AI tools on their programming practices. The research will include rigorous data collection and analysis to provide insights into the potential relationship between AI and programming and to identify how programmers can leverage AI tools to enhance their work.
