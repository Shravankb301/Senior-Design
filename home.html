The Impact of Artificial Intelligence:

The Future of Programmers with Autocomplete

About Our Project

Artificial Intelligence (AI) is a specific area that enables computers to simulate human intelligence when they complete tasks. Developers use this advanced algorithm-based AI knowledge to create features and software programs that assist humans in either fixing or identifying problems. Auto-complete tools incorporating machine learning (ML) can be applied to almost any activity involving analyzing or interpreting natural language code. It consists of various models with different custom algorithms from multiple datasets to solve a particular task ranging from generation to classification. Our objective is to examine the different AI/ML coding tools to determine their efficacy for programmers' day-to-day work. Additionally, through use case comparisons, we seek to determine the depth of knowledge required to utilize these tools and maintain high-performance levels. Could the equivalent of a programmer who earned an associate degree perform at the same level as a graduate or doctoral graduate with these AI/ML tools?
